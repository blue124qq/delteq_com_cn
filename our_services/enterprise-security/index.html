Our Solutions

    • Identify and access management
At Digital Technology, our identify and access management solutions allow businesses to get the evolving requirements around mobile devices and cloud apps by enabling secure access to online resources and protecting the digital interactions of partners, employees, and clients.

    • Data Encryption
You can protect your business’s sensitive information wherever it is transmitted or is resides with data-at-rest and data-in-motion encryption solutions. Your business must use encryption to save sensitive data to reduce the risk posed by malicious attacks, hackers, and insider threats.

The risk is transferred from the data itself to the cryptographic keys when encryption is used. The ability to securely manage, use keys, and the store is important. You can secure your cryptographic keys with business key management, centralized crypto resource management, and the use of a hardware root of trust.

You can increase control and security in the cloud, migrate to new services with flexibility, and fully leverage the cloud’s business agility.
